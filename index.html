DEPARTMENT VISION

TO BE RECOGNIZED AS THE PREMIER CENTER IN THE FIELD OF ELECTRONICS ENGINEERING EDUCATION, PRODUCING TECHNICALLY COMPETENT AND SOCIALLY RESPONSIBLE PROFESSIONALS.

DEPARTMENT MISSION

  • M1: TO IMPART TECHNICAL EDUCATION IN LINE WITH EFFECTIVE TEACHING LEARNING PROCESS TO MEET THE CURRENT INDUSTRIAL AND SOCIETAL NEEDS.
  • M2: TO STRENGTHEN ACADEMIC INFRASTRUCTURE BY PROVIDING STATE-OF-THE-ART LABORATORIES THAT ALIGN WITH TRENDS IN TECHNOLOGY.
  • M3: TO PROVIDE A CONDUCIVE ENVIRONMENT TO ENCOURAGE STUDENTS TO DEVELOP ENTREPRENEURSHIP SKILLS AND LIFE-LONG LEARNING.
